Changing the Search Engine on Microsoft HoloLens

  1. First, open Microsoft Edge
  2. Type in "edge://settings"
  3. On the left side, go to "Privacy, search, and services"
  4. Scroll all the way to the bottom and select "Address bar and search"
  5. Click on "Manage search engines"
  6. Click "add"
  7. For Search engine, type in "https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/". The keyword should be "pubmed.gov", and the url should be "http://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov?term=%s"
  8. Click "add"
  9. To make the search engine default, click on the three dots next to the specific search engine and select "Make default".
