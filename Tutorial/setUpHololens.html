Setting up the HoloLens

If you do not have the password for the HoloLens, you will have to factory reset it

Use the link above to reset it or follow (1) below

1. Reset the HoloLens

  1. First, unplug any cables that are connected to the HoloLens
  2. Next, hold volume down (the - button on the right side of your head and the power (the circle button hear the bottom of your head button for 15 seconds
  3. At this point, the HoloLens battery indicator should only have the middle button turned on
  4. Connect the device to your PC
  5. Open device manager and make sure that the Hololens is under Universal Serial Bus devices
  6. Install the Advanced Recovery Companion in the Windows store onto your PC
  7. Open the Advanced Recovery Companion and follow the steps there to completely wipe the HoloLens

2. Setting up your account

At this point, you should turn on the HoloLens and follow its instructions to create a user account on there.

Make sure that you allow privacy settings about voice and Cortana at this time to make sure that they can function properly.

Tips

  • In order to know what commands you can say, state "What can I say?"
  • Tap your wrist or say "Go to start" in order to access the start menu
  • Say "close" to close any application
  • Say "follow me" for an app to follow you, and "stop following" to place the app
  • For more helpful tips, look at the tips app in the start menu. We highly recommend it!
